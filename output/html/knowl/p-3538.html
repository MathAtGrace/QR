The graph is reproduced at right. We can choose any two points on the line to compute its slope. Using points \(P\) and \(Q\) as shown, we find that

\begin{equation*} m = \frac{\Delta C}{\Delta t}= \frac{9}{3}=3 \end{equation*}

The slope of the line is \(3\text{.}\)
